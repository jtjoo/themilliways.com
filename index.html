themilliways

Alternative-rock based musician create unique soundscapes inspired by ambient, experimental rock, shoegazing, and post-rock.

themilliways, as known as South Korean artist Jintae Joo, crafts emotive instrumental soundscapes that explore the depths of human experience. His debut single "3rd air" launched on December 2021. The music featured on Seotaiji's 8th album Seotaiji 8th Atomos teaser announcement video in 2008, made over 200k music fans excited. This venture follows Joo's two-decade journey as a composer and audio engineer in the Korean music industry. Drawing guitar play on his own style and innovative effects, themilliways pushes the boundaries of expressive sound and dare experiment, continues to translate complex emotions into captivating auditory journeys, inviting listeners to explore vast internal landscapes through the power of music.

"Disappear"

2024.12.08.

One day I was going through very difficult times in my life. I felt like I wanted to disappear from this world. After releasing "3rd air", I was struggling with various thoughts about my life and future. One day I grabbed guitar, played as I felt. Depression, anxiety, loneliness, and hope all mixed together in my mind.
