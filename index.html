themilliways, as known as South Korean artist Jintae Joo, crafts emotive instrumental soundscapes that explore the depths of human experience. His debut single "3rd air" launched on December 2021. This venture follows Joo's two-decade journey as a composer and audio engineer in the Korean music industry. Drawing guitar play on his own style and innovative effects, themilliways pushes the boundaries of expressive sound and dare experiment, continues to translate complex emotions into captivating auditory journeys, inviting listeners to explore vast internal landscapes through the power of music.
